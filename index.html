The Bugatti Chiron Tourbillon features a 30º inclined tourbillon, the first time Jacob & Co. has ever used an inclined tourbillon.

Specialities

  • The watch has a 30º inclined flying tourbillon that completes one revolution every minute.
  • The watch is powered by two barrels, providing a 60-hour power reserve.
  • This is the first time Jacob & Co. has used an inclined tourbillon.
  • The watch is made up of 578 components, and has 51 jewels.
  • The watch has a puristic design, with two "exhausts" framing the engine block.
  • The watch has a sapphire crystal window that allows the movement to be clearly visible.
